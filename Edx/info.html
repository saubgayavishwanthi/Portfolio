1. Graduate Diploma in Software Engineering-GDSE

    • Level:Diploma
    • Institute:Institute of Java and Software Engineering - IJSE
    • Intakes : may
    • Duration : 2years

    Entry Requirement

    3 Pass Grades at G.C.E. Advanced Level Examination in any stream of study. OR, (B) 2 Pass Grades for Mathematics and English at G.C.E. Ordinary Level Examination + Pass the Aptitude Test.

    • Multi-Threaded Programing
    • Database Management Systems
    • Introduction to UX Engineering
